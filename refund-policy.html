Refund Policy

30-Day Money Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our authentic Assam tea. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee from the date of delivery.

Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund, please ensure that:

  • The product is unused and in the same condition as received
  • The original packaging is intact and undamaged
  • The refund request is made within 30 days of delivery
  • You provide proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@saapati.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  3. Include photos of the product and packaging if applicable
  4. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours

Refund Process

Once your refund request is approved:

  • We'll provide instructions for returning the product
  • Return shipping costs will be covered by us for defective items
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Products that have been opened or used
  • Items returned after the 30-day period
  • Products damaged due to customer misuse
  • Items without original packaging

Exchanges

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, we'll be happy to exchange it for the correct item. Please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order to arrange an exchange.
