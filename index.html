Perfect Cup Guide

Learn how to brew the perfect cup of Saapati chai

1

Boil Water

Heat 1 cup of filtered water in a pan until it boils vigorously.

2

Add Tea

Add half a teaspoon (2.5g) of Saapati chai to the boiling water.

3

Add Milk

Pour in half a cup of milk. Add sugar or sweetener to taste.

4

Simmer

Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes. Allow the flavors to blend beautifully.

5

Strain & Enjoy

Strain into your favorite cup and enjoy the fresh, authentic taste of Assam.

From Assam to Your Cup — The Real Taste of Chai

We grew up with real chai.

I grew up in Namrup, Assam — surrounded by tea gardens where chai is part of everyday life. Strong aroma, deep color, and full-bodied taste.

Chai had lost its soul.

When I moved to cities, the tea lacked strength, color, and real taste. It felt like people weren’t drinking Assam tea anymore.

That’s when Saapati was born.

Not as a business, but as a mission to bring back real chai.

What makes us different

  • 100% Assam CTC Tea
  • Strong, kadak taste
  • No mixing. No dilution.
  • Honest, full-bodied chai

Because chai is not just a drink. It’s a feeling of home.
